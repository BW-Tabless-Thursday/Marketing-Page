Save Your Tabs

Save all your tabs in an ordered, easy-to-use fashion. Rather than a horizontal mess of unorder tabs, tabs become a to-do list based on what you set as important or otherwise.

Preview Saved Sites

Don't remember what a tab looked like? Get a sneak peek of what you had going on with each tab. Simply selecting a tab will give you an image of what the site looks like.

Notes, Notes, Notes

One of the biggest advantages of using Tabless Thursday is the addition of notes for each tab saved. Notes allow you to set goals, reminders, or even descriptions for each tab you save.

Share With Friends

Planning is always better with other people. With Tabless Thursday, friends or colleagues can login and view your tab lists, notes, and other info you'd like to share with them.
